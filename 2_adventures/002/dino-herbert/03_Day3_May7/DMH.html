As an architect, I am often asked about why I am focused on IT and programming. Actually, they are really quite similar....

"The practice of architecture is the most delightful of all pursuits."

"I think that it's extraordinarily important that we in computer science keep fun in computing. When it started out it was an awful lot of fun. We began to feel as if we really were responsible for the successful error-free perfect use of these machines. I don't think we are. I think we're responsible for stretching them, setting them off in new directions and keeping fun in the house."

Alan J. Perlis

Jaquelin T. Robertson
